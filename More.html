Mother Gothel:In a tower tall, Rapunzel's call, Mother Gothel's hold, deceitful thrall. Bound by fear, but love unseen, Rapunzel dreams of a world serene.

Pascal, with his bright green scales, is Rapunzel's loyal companion, always by her side. His silent gestures speak volumes, showing love and support in every small movement. With a playful spirit and keen intelligence, Pascal adds joy to Rapunzel's tower-bound life. Together, they share a bond that transcends words, a friendship as vibrant as Pascal's colors.

Flynn Rider:In a tower tall and grand, Rapunzel's hair so long and grand, Flynn Rider came along, with a charm that was so strong. Together they found their fate, hearts entwined, never to separate. In love's embrace, their story sings, forever bound by golden strings.
